Malawi Tourism Explorer

The name "Malawi Tourism Explorer" was chosen because the site will showcase some of the beautiful places people can visit in Malawi.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide a simple guide to different tourist destinations people can visit.

Scenarios

  • Which place can I visit, and why?
  • What are some of the interesting things I can see at that site?
